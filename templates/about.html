Navigation Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts will only work on latest Firefox, Chrome and Edge.

Key Action
Alt+Shift+1 New Releases
Alt+Shift+2 Podcasts
Alt+Shift+3 Discover
Alt+Shift+4 Play Queue
Alt+Shift+5 Favorites
Alt+Shift+6 History
Alt+Shift+8 About

Player Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts are active only when the Player is running.

Key Action
Space Play/Pause Player
Delete Stop and Close Player
Arrow Left (←) Skip 10s Back
Arrow Right (→) Skip 10s Forwards
Plus (+) Speed Up Playback Rate
Minus (-) Slow Down Playback Rate
Shift+Tab Play Next Episode in Queue
