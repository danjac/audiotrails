Player Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts are available when the Player is running.

Action Key
Toggle Play/Pause Spacebar
Skip Backwards 10s Left Arrow
Skip Forwards 10s Right Arrow
Increase Playback Rate alt+'+'
Decrease Playback Rate alt+'-'
Reset Default Playback Rate alt+'0'
Close Player (if running) alt+shift+'x'
Reload Player (if running) alt+shift+'r'
