Player Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts are active only when the Player is running.

Key Action
Space Play/Pause Player
Delete Stop and Close Player
Arrow Left (←) Skip 10s Back
Arrow Right (→) Skip 10s Forwards
Plus (+) Speed Up Playback Rate
Minus (-) Slow Down Playback Rate
Shift+Tab Play Next Episode in Queue
