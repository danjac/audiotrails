Navigation Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts should be used in combination with Alt+Shift keys on Windows and Linux devices (Chrome/Firefox/Edge) and Ctrl+Option keys on Mac devices (Chrome/Firefox/Safari).

Key Action
1 New Releases
2 Podcasts
3 Discover
4 Play Queue
5 Favorites
6 History
7 Settings
8 About
k Search

Action Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts should be used in combination with Alt+Shift keys on Windows and Linux devices (Chrome/Firefox/Edge) and Ctrl+Option keys on Mac devices (Chrome/Firefox/Safari).

Key Action
p Open episode in Player
q Add/Remove episode from Play Queue
v Add/Remove episode from Favorites
w Follow/Stop following podcast

Player Shortcuts

These keyboard shortcuts are active only when the Player is running.

Key Action
Space Play/Pause Player
Delete Stop and Close Player
Arrow Left (←) Skip 10s Back
Arrow Right (→) Skip 10s Forwards
Plus (+) Speed Up Playback Rate
Minus (-) Slow Down Playback Rate
Zero (0) Reset Playback Rate to x1.0
